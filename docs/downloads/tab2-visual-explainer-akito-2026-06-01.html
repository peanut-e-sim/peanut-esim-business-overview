3The Refuel mechanic

When a subscriber hits 80% of their pool, the app surfaces a prompt: "+1 GB for $4.99." This is not a bug or dark pattern — it's the core breakage-as-revenue mechanic. We call it Refuel. The attach rate (% of subscribers who tap Yes) is the single biggest upside lever in the model.

How the Refuel prompt fires

$4.99 charged, 1 GB added instantly
→
Revenue booked
$4.99 top-up at ~$1.34 COGS
Why this matters: A refuel sale has near-identical margin to the base plan (we pay ~$1.34/GB wholesale). At $4.99 per GB, the gross margin is ~73%. At scale with 50,000 subscribers, even a 5% attach rate is 50,000 × 5% × $4.99 = $12,475/month in near-pure-margin revenue.

4Where crew actually fly: the Geo Mix

We pay our wholesale supplier per GB delivered — and the price depends on which country the crew is in. The 75/20/5 split is the new base case, replacing the older 60/25/15. It reflects Emirates-realistic routes: most flying is intra-Asia + Middle East, which are cheap markets.

GB consumption by region tier

75% of all consumed GBs land in cheap Tier-A markets — that's the cost advantage.

Wholesale cost breakdown

The blended $1.34/GB is a weighted average of these three tiers.

Tier A · cheap regions · 75% $0.85/GB
Asia: JP, SG, HK, KR, TH · Middle East: AE, QA · UK, MY
Tier B · mid regions · 20% $1.40/GB
Europe, AU, NZ, Canada, select SE Asia
Tier C · expensive regions · 5% $8.50/GB
US, sub-Saharan Africa, South America, cruise markets
Blended cost (75/20/5) $1.34/GB
The math:
0.75 × $0.85 = $0.64
0.20 × $1.40 = $0.28
0.05 × $8.50 = $0.43
Total = $1.34/GB blended

5Why we revised the Geo Mix

The original model used 60/25/15. It felt conservative — 15% in expensive Tier-C countries was too high given Emirates' actual route map. The revised 75/20/5 brings the blended cost from $2.13 down to $1.34. That's a 37% reduction in modelled COGS.

Old vs New: blended wholesale cost per GB

Shifting Tier-C share from 15% to 5% is the key move. Tier-C at $8.50/GB is an outlier that dominates the blend when over-weighted.
