5The Strategic Insight

The single biggest takeaway from Tab 4 — relevant now, even though the tool is parked.

Pricing is NOT the lever. Supplier negotiation is. Our current 70% margins are good. 93% margins are extraordinary. The path from one to the other is not a UI change or a pricing war — it is a single supplier conversation that requires ~5K users and a credible alternative in our back pocket. Don't drop retail prices to acquire users. Build the user base, then negotiate.
What the simulator actually tells us: At current wholesale costs, our retail prices are already well-optimized. The next 23 percentage points of margin improvement come entirely from procurement, not pricing. This is why Tab 15 (Procurement Options) is more urgent than Tab 4.
The trap to avoid: Do not use this simulator to justify a promotional price drop. A 20% discount to "get users faster" costs $2.52/user/month in lost margin at current rates — and you would need to re-raise prices after, creating churn. The math never recovers at sub-$17.99 Premium pricing.
