The persona behind this plan

Senior crew or pilots on long-haul routes who want a premium, generous data plan with full rollover across two months. 🎛️Sensitivity — what fixes this?

Current: 50 GB / $39.99. Two fixes: cut pool to 35 GB, or raise price to $59.99.

Recommended fix before launch: Either cut pool to 35 GB (COGS = $28.14 → $11.85 net at 60%) or raise price to $59.99 ($59.99 − $40.20 = $19.79 net). The price raise is cleaner — it reinforces premium positioning. A 35 GB cap risks disappointing crew who were sold on "50 GB."

🔍The honest read

RED FLAG: Currently breaks even at 60% consumption. This plan loses money at average usage. The math is clear: 50 GB × 60% × $1.34/GB = $40.20 in COGS vs. $39.99 in revenue. You are paying your wholesale supplier more than the user is paying you. This must be fixed before launch. The recommended action is raising the price to $59.99 — which keeps the plan premium, adds $20 in net contribution, and is defensible given the 50 GB pool + 100% 2-month carry. Do not launch First at $39.99 with a 50 GB pool.
