👤Who it's for

The persona behind this plan

Senior crew or pilots on long-haul routes who want a premium, generous data plan. Example: Amira, a First Class cabin supervisor flying DXB–AUS–DXB monthly, wants 25 GB for peace of mind. She may stream occasionally on layovers and video-call home, but most of her data use happens in hotel WiFi zones. The 40% average consumption rate reflects that pilots and senior crew spend much of their time on company WiFi and don't constantly stream.

Pool: 25 GB. Consumption: 40% (10 GB). Refuel attach: 40% — senior crew who do run out want it topped up immediately.
