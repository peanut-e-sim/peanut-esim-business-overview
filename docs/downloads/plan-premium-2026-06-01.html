👤Who it's for

The persona behind this plan

The most common crew profile: flying 4-8 legs a month, using data steadily across destinations, and wanting the convenience of a monthly plan without overpaying. Example: Sarah, a Premium Economy cabin crew member who flies DXB–LHR–DXB twice a month and does occasional layovers in Singapore. She uses ~5-6 GB, doesn't need 25 GB, and appreciates the 50% carry that rolls unused data forward once. Premium Economy is designed precisely for her — and she's 13% of your user mix.

🔍The honest read

Premium Economy: The workhorse. Most profitable per user before refuel uplift — and refuel adds meaningfully. $11.15 net contribution per user per month is the best ratio of price-to-margin in the subscription stack. The 8% refuel attach rate (highest of any tier) reflects that 10 GB occasionally isn't enough for active layover travelers — which is a feature, not a bug. Every refuel top-up at $4.99 adds $3.65 net. At 13% mix, this is the tier to push as the "upgrade from Roster Bundle" destination. If you can shift 5 percentage points of Economy users to Premium Economy, your blended margin improves visibly.
