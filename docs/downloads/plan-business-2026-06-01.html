👤Who it's for

The persona behind this plan

Long-haul crew who fly transatlantic or transpacific routes and spend significant time in data-heavy destinations. They value peace of mind over penny-pinching: $29.99 for 25 GB with full rollover means they never worry about data mid-trip. Example: Marco, a Business Class FA who flies DXB–JFK–DXB twice a month, uses ~8 GB normally but occasionally hits 20 GB during long layovers in New York. Business is the recommended plan because it optimizes the carry mechanic — 70% of unused data rolls over once, protecting heavy months.

🔍The honest read

Business: Highest absolute contribution. The star of the ladder. $18.93 net per user per month is exceptional. The apparent paradox — 25 GB pool but only 33% average use — is intentional design. Business users burst: their average is 8 GB but their peak months hit 20+ GB. The 70% carry means they never feel ripped off in a light month, so churn stays low. Low churn × high margin = compounding LTV. If you push one tier to new subscribers, push Business. The "recommended" badge on the pricing page should do real work here.
