Here's how Peanut eSIM makes money.

We sell crew a monthly data pool. Most crew don't use all of it. The unused gigs are pure margin — we never pay our supplier for them. Layer that across 7 tiers and a 75/20/5 country mix, and the business prints predictable cash.

1The 7-tier ladder

Every tier is anchored to a cabin class. The price ladder mirrors the seat ladder — crew already know it, so it sells itself. First Suite is the anchor at the top; Business is what we recommend most.

2How we actually make profit: breakage

Crew rarely burn their full pool. They pay for 10 GB, use ~4 GB, and the carryover goes one of four places: used on the next trip, used at home in UAE, gifted to family, or expired as pure margin. Every unused gig is margin we never owed our supplier — unless they redeem it intelligently.

The trick: We charged for 10 GB but only paid our supplier for ~4 GB used this month + at most 3 GB carried into next month. The remaining 3 GB? Expires as pure margin. Below: what happens to those 3 carried GB.

100 GB of crew data — where does it physically get used?

Take 100 GB of total crew data consumption in a month. Based on the 75/20/5 split, here's where every GB physically lands and what our wholesale supplier charges us:

Where it lands GB share × $/GB = cost
Tier-A (Asia, Africa) · cheap 75 GB × $0.85 $63.75
Tier-B (Europe, AU/NZ) · medium 20 GB × $1.40 $28.00
Tier-C (USA, Cruise, S. America) · expensive 5 GB × $8.50 $42.50
Total cost for 100 GB 100 GB avg $134.25
Per-GB blended cost = $134.25 ÷ 100 GB $1.34 / GB

The Tier-C trap: only 5 GB lands in Tier-C, but it costs us $42.50 — a third of our total bill. A small shift in mix (5% → 10% Tier-C) jumps blended cost from $1.34 → $1.77/GB (+32%). That's why we keep harping on the geo mix assumption.

ONE-USER VIEW

Aiko (Premium) consumes 4 GB this month — what does it cost us?

Aiko's data lands across the same 75/20/5 mix. So her 4 GB breaks down:

  • 3.0 GB in Tier-A (Bangkok, Mumbai layovers) × $0.85 = $2.55
  • 0.8 GB in Tier-B (Paris stopover) × $1.40 = $1.12
  • 0.2 GB in Tier-C (NYC layover) × $8.50 = $1.70
  • Total supplier cost for Aiko: $5.37

Aiko pays Peanut $18.99 → we keep ~$12.62 gross. Her single NYC layover (0.2 GB) cost us more than her entire 3 GB in Bangkok combined. That's the Tier-C lesson in one user.

Refuel Attach = 30%
Of all subscriber-months, ~30% include a Refuel top-up purchase ($4.99 for an extra 1 GB). This is a weighted average across all subscription tiers (Premium ~20%, Business ~25%, First ~40%, Cockpit ~15%, etc.). Each refuel adds $3.65 of contribution on top. Refuel attach is one of the most-leveraged levers in the model — every 5pp shift = ~$182/mo per 1K users.

ALL ASSUMPTIONS Actual spend = $0 today. These get refined as real costs hit (Tab 13).
Payment processing $1.20

What Stripe and Apple/Google take from every transaction. On a $13 ticket:

  • Stripe: 2.9% + $0.30/txn ≈ $0.68
  • Apple/Google IAP: 15–30% (avoided where possible)
  • Our hybrid model (Decision 01) keeps most users on Stripe
Biggest line. At 100K users = $120K/mo.
Customer support $0.30

Cost to help users with refunds, install issues, account problems.

  • 5% of users contact support / month
  • $6 per contact (AI-first via Intercom Fin or similar)
  • 5% × $6 = $0.30
Doubles if activation failure rate jumps 1% → 5%.
Cloud / API $0.15

Backend infrastructure.

  • Database + auth (Supabase / Firebase): ~$0.05
  • eSIM Access API calls (provision / top-up / status): ~$0.07
  • Push, analytics, logging: ~$0.03
Scales sub-linearly. At 100K users ≈ $0.10/user.
Hosting + dev tools $0.10

Flat operating costs that don't scale per-user.

  • Vercel/Netlify marketing site (~$20/mo)
  • Apple Developer Program ($99/yr ÷ 12)
  • Sentry/Datadog monitoring (~$50/mo)
  • GitHub Pro, Figma, design tools (~$30/mo)
At 100 users ≈ $1/user. At 100K ≈ rounding error.
SMS / OTP $0.05

One-time password texts for sign-in and crew verification.

  • Twilio / Vonage cost: ~$0.0075 per SMS
  • ~6 SMS per user per year
  • 6 × $0.0075 ÷ 12 = $0.05
International SMS (UAE/India) can be 3× more. Soft estimate.
