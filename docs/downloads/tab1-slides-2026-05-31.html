09 / 11
Quarterly Watchlist — Revisit These 4
01

Geo Mix Calibration

Actual crew route distribution (EK network) vs. assumed 60/25/15. A 5-pt shift to Tier C costs ~$0.40/GB blended. Review after first 500 activations.

02

Refuel Attach Rate (Akito's lever)

Currently assumed at 10% of active subs. Akito's in-app push & Crew Recs gamification could move this to 18–22%. Every 1-pt move = ~$0.06/user/mo revenue lift.

03

Support Cost at 5K-User Inflection

$0.30/user is modeled on AI-first support + low escalation. At 5K users the support queue may require a part-time human. Watch ticket-to-user ratio.

04

Wholesale Cost (Direct Supplier Path)

Current model uses Samurai sub-reseller rates (40% flat margin). Signing direct would yield 3.5× net on data COGS. Trigger: 2K active users or $10K MRR.
