👤Who it's for

The persona behind this plan

Crew who find monthly subscriptions wasteful but still want automatic connectivity for every flight. Example: Kenji, a part-time FA flying 4-6 legs a month, doesn't want to think about eSIM — he uploads his roster, the plan fires for each flight, and he's billed per leg at $3.99. The auto-cap at $39.99/mo (10 flights) means it never blows up. Roster Bundle is the entry funnel for crew who are "roster-first" thinkers.

Assumes 5% refuel attach — crew flying multiple legs sometimes blow their 3 GB mid-trip and need a top-up.

🔍The honest read

Roster Bundle: Loss-leader anchor — barely worth running on its own. At $2.43/flight net and ~5 flights/month, you're making $12.15/user vs. $11.15 for Premium Economy on a higher price point. The math isn't the point. Roster Bundle exists as a gateway: it captures crew who won't commit to monthly billing, builds habit around Peanut eSIM's roster-upload UX (our core wedge vs. Crew SIM), and creates a natural upgrade path. Run it, but make every session include an upgrade nudge.
