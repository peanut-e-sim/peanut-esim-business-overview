Roster Bundle

Auto-armed per flight leg — the crew-native entry point for light flyers who think in flights, not months. ~5 flights/mo = $19.95 ARPU.
Updated 2026-06-01
$19.95 / month (~5 flights × $3.99)

👤Who it's for

The persona behind this plan

Crew who find monthly subscriptions wasteful but still want automatic connectivity for every flight. Example: Kenji, a part-time FA flying 4-6 legs a month, doesn't want to think about eSIM — he uploads his roster, the plan fires for each flight at $3.99/leg, and he's billed per leg. The auto-cap at $39.99/mo (10 flights) means it never blows up. Roster Bundle is the entry funnel for crew who are "roster-first" thinkers.

Model uses average 5 flights/mo = $19.95 ARPU. Pool is 5 GB total across those flights (1 GB/flight avg), 100% consumed (PAYG model — all delivered data is used). Refuel attach 10%.

📊Pool vs. consumption

5 GB pool — 100% consumed (per-flight PAYG — no carry-forward)

5 GB fully consumed across ~5 flights. No carry-forward on this plan — unused data from flight A doesn't roll into flight B. Because all purchased data is consumed, there is no breakage. This is a clean PAYG model at the monthly level.
