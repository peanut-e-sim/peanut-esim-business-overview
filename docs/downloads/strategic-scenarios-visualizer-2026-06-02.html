5The 8-Feature Moat

Crew SIM has a 41-country price list. We have that PLUS an 8-feature stack that creates real switching costs. We compete on the bundle, not on price.

CrewLink Roster Upload

High switching cost. No competitor replicates this. Ties us to crew workflow.

Gifting Carryover

Viral mechanism. Unused data becomes a gift — triggers word-of-mouth.

UAE Redemption

Use your eSIM data at home in Dubai via same-SIM dynamic allocation. No other provider offers this for crew.

Crew Recs (Michelin-style)

Content moat. Crew-curated destination intel. Impossible to copy without the community.

Same-SIM Dynamic Allocation

One eSIM, multiple country pools. Better UX than juggling multiple plans.

60% Cheaper Than 1 GB Packets

Subscription pools beat per-GB pricing for frequent fliers. The math always wins.

AI Support

Instant install help, top-up, refunds via AI chat. Crew SIM is email-only.

Annual Cockpit Prepay

Locked retention. One payment, one year. The user is off the market entirely.

The big insight
Bundle, not price.

Crew SIM has 41 countries on a price list. We have 41 countries plus 8 features that create daily-life utility, viral mechanics, and retention lock. Our moat is not $1 cheaper data. It is the 8-feature stack.

7The 4-Step Playbook if Crew SIM Attacks

Sequential response. Do not skip ahead. Most attacks resolve at Step 2 without any price change.

1
Days 1-7

Don't react publicly

Watch the move. Do not post, do not announce. Gather data on what they actually changed. Knee-jerk reactions signal weakness.

2
Week 2-4

Assess actual impact

Track new signups and churn rate. If neither moves in Week 3, ignore completely. Most competitive noise has zero measurable impact on crew signups.

3
Only if signups drop

Spring Promotion

Looks organic, not reactive. Never cut posted prices. Options: 1 month free Premium for new crew. Refer 3, get Cockpit at 50% for Year 1.

4
Parallel track

Aggressive retention

Target at-risk users individually. Premium users 6+ months: push to Cockpit annual (locks them). Heavy users: free CrewLink upgrade. No blanket discounts.

8Economy is the Exposed Tier

The only tier where Crew SIM's price and our floor are within striking distance of each other.

Hard floor — do not cross
$4.71

Economy minimum viable price = COGS $3.36 + OPEX $1.35. Any lower and we lose money on every Economy purchase. Crew SIM reference price: $4.99. Our cushion: $0.28.

Why Economy is uniquely vulnerable
  • PAYG structure means no subscription lock-in
  • Price-sensitive users comparison-shop more
  • Cushion ($0.28) leaves almost zero maneuver room
  • Cannot deploy Spring Promotion (no recurring relationship to leverage)
Monitoring trigger: If Crew SIM ever lists Economy-equivalent at below $4.99, we match immediately. If they list below $4.71, we flag for a pricing architecture review — we cannot profitably follow that low.
