Who it's for

The persona behind this plan

Pilots, pursers, and senior long-haul crew who fly year-round and want a single annual payment that covers everything. They're budget-disciplined but appreciate value — $299/yr with 12 GB/mo equivalent means consistent data without monthly renewal friction. Example: Captain Nakamura, flying the DXB–NRT route monthly for the year. He wants data for navigation apps, hotel entertainment, and family calls. Annual pay means no renewal friction, no monthly billing surprises.

Model: $24.92/mo equivalent, 12 GB/mo pool, 60% consumed = 7.2 GB/mo, 15% refuel attach.

▴ CONSUMPTION SENSITIVITY — Cockpit fragility flagged by council 2026-06-02

The 35% gross margin above holds only at the modeled 60% pool consumption (7.2 GB/mo). Cockpit subscribers are heavy users by definition (they paid annually upfront for predictable data), so they're disproportionately likely to push past the 60% assumption. Stress table:

Open decision: Add an overage policy (per-GB charge above 12 GB pool), a hard data cap, or simply track real Cockpit consumption in beta and revisit. Without one of these, Cockpit is the most exposed tier to silent consumption creep. See Decision 00C · council follow-up item #1 on main site.
