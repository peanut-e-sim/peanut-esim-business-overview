Who it's for

The persona behind this plan

The most senior crew: pilots, pursers, and long-haul First Class crew who want a prestige data plan with a generous pool. Example: Captain Tanaka, flying DXB–NRT–DXB routes monthly. He wants guaranteed data — but most of his time in Japan is in hotels with WiFi, and in-flight he's on the cockpit system. Despite a 40 GB pool, he realistically consumes about 13 GB. First Suite exists as the price anchor: it makes Business look like great value while generating the highest absolute profit in the ladder.

Pool: 40 GB. Consumption: 33% (13.2 GB). Refuel attach: 20%.
