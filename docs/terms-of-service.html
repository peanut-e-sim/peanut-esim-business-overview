2.Service Description

2.1 What We Provide

Peanut eSIM is a digital reseller of prepaid mobile data plans delivered as an embedded SIM ("eSIM") profile for use on compatible smartphones, tablets, and other connected devices while traveling internationally. We do not own or operate any mobile network. We obtain wholesale data capacity from a licensed eSIM aggregator (currently Samurai Wifi and its upstream Mobile Network Operators ("MNOs")) and resell that capacity to end users in convenient prepaid bundles via our mobile application, website, and supported in-app billing channels.

2.2 What an eSIM Plan Is

An eSIM plan ("Plan") is a digital good. It is delivered as a QR code or activation link that, when installed on a compatible device, provisions a temporary mobile data profile valid for a specific geography (country or regional bundle), data quota (e.g., 1 GB, 5 GB, unlimited capped), and validity period (e.g., 7 days, 30 days). A Plan typically does not include voice minutes, SMS, or a local phone number unless explicitly stated at the point of sale.

2.3 No Telecommunications Carrier Status

Peanut eSIM is a data reseller and value-added service provider, not a licensed telecommunications carrier in any jurisdiction. We do not provide emergency calling services. Where local law (including UAE Telecommunications and Digital Government Regulatory Authority ("TDRA") rules) prohibits a non-licensed entity from offering certain telecommunications services to local residents, this Service is offered solely as a travel data service for use outside the User's country of permanent residence, except as expressly permitted by local law.
